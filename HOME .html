E-waste management refers to the processes and practices involved in managing electronic waste (e-waste) in an environmentally responsible manner. E-waste includes discarded electronic devices, such as:- Computers
- Smartphones
- Televisions
- Printers
- Batteries
- Cables
- Other electronic components
