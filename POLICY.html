The e-waste management policy provides for, among others, enactment of specific legislation for proper E-waste management and disposal to safe guard human life.

The Government of Uganda recognized the challenges posed by e-waste and put in place an enabling environment to facilitate its sustainable.
Against this background, Government of Uganda developed the E-Waste Management. Policy whose overall objective is to guide, promote and ensure the safe protection.
